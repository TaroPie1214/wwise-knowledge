[AkOutputDeviceInfo](struct_ak_output_device_info.html) & | *out\_outputDeviceInfo* | ) | const | | pure virtual |  Retrieve information about the output (audio) device that the caller is routed to.  When called from a plug-in placed on a bus, the bus hierarchy is traversed upward until the master bus is reached. The audio device connected to this master bus is then queried. When called from a source plug-in, the source's output bus is the starting point of the traversal. When called from an audio device or audio device effect plug-in, that audio device is queried.   |  |  | | --- | --- | |  | **警告:** During Init, the plug-in may not be connected to the pipeline yet.
