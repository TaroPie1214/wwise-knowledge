## 详细描述

Multi-channel volume definitions and services. Always associated with an [AkChannelConfig](struct_ak_channel_config.html). In the case of standard configurations, the volume items ordering match the bit ordering in the channel mask, except for the LFE which is skipped and placed at the end of the volume array. See [使用 Speaker Matrix Callback 定制高级混音](goingfurther_speakermatrixcallback.html) for an example of how to manipulate speaker volume vectors/matrices.
