### SoundBanks Settings 对话框

您可以在 SoundBanks Settings（音频包设置）对话框中为自己的 SoundBank 设定自定义设置。自定义用户设置时，必须取消沿用相应的[工程设置](../../../01-工程/08-Project-Settings/03-SoundBanks-选项卡/00-SoundBanks-选项卡.md "SoundBanks 选项卡")。

SoundBanks Settings 对话框分为两个不同的选项卡：

- [“SoundBanks Settings — SoundBanks 选项卡”一节](01-SoundBanks-Settings-—-SoundBanks-选项卡.md "SoundBanks Settings — SoundBanks 选项卡")
- [“SoundBanks Settings - External Sources 选项卡”一节](02-SoundBanks-Settings-External-Sources-选项卡.md "SoundBanks Settings - External Sources 选项卡")
