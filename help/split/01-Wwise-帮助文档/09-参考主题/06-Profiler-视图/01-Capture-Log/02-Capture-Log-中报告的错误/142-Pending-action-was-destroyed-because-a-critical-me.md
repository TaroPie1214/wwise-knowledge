#### Pending action was destroyed because a critical memory allocation failed.

“关键内存分配失败，已终止待处理操作”。内存不足，无法安排具有延迟的 Event（事件）操作或进行重复计数的 Sequence/Random Container（序列/随机容器）。将不执行后续操作。在此之前应会出现“[“Memory allocation failed.”一节](115-Memory-allocation-failed..md "Memory allocation failed.")”错误。
