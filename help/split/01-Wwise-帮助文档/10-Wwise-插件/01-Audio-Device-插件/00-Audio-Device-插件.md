## Audio Device 插件

Audio Device plug-ins receive the final mixed audio samples at the end of the Wwise pipeline and pass them to the part of the deployment platform that processes audio, referred to as an endpoint. The Audio Device plug-in formats the data according to the requirements of the endpoint.
